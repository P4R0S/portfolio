Which interactive elements do you want?

Select all that appeal to you — these will be layered onto your Figma CV design

A

Scroll-reveal animations

Each section fades/slides in as you scroll down. The sidebar animates in from the left, main content from the right. Feels premium and polished.

B

Hover highlights on rows

Experience and education entries subtly glow or shift background on hover. Makes it feel interactive without being distracting.

C

Download PDF button

A prominent button that downloads the actual PDF of your CV. Could be sticky in the corner or at the top of the page.

D

Print-clean view toggle

A toggle that switches between the stylized web view and a clean, print-ready version — same layout as your Figma design, no decorative effects.

E

Sticky section progress dots

Small dot indicators on the side that show which section you're reading and let you jump between them. Common in long-form portfolio pages.

F

Expandable experience entries

Each job/education entry shows a summary, click to expand and see full bullet points. Keeps the page compact and scannable.
