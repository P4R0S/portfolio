Just a blinking cursor and a subtle hint. Clean and minimal — the user discovers it themselves. No fanfare.

B — Auto-typed greeting

On load, 2–3 lines type themselves out (typewriter effect), then the cursor lands. Feels alive. Invites interaction naturally.

C — Pre-run demo

Arrives with whoami already executed and its output visible — shows visitors exactly what the terminal does before they touch it. Then the cursor waits for their input.
