Design details

  • Tilt: 2° clockwise rotation — "floating window" feel, not perfectly rigid
  • Scanlines: subtle CRT scanline overlay on the widget only
  • Glow: faint amber box-shadow around the terminal border
  • Entrance: slides in from right + fade as hero enters viewport (Framer Motion whileInView)
  • Click-to-focus: clicking anywhere in the widget focuses the hidden input
  • Mobile: terminal stacks below the text column, tilt removed, slightly smaller font
